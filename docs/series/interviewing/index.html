Prepare your strategy [Tips minus the bullshit]

#Tip –> After getting an interview, the first thing you should do is assess yourself. Ask yourself the following questions – How familiar are you with algorithms? How much practice have you done? And most importantly, How well you can explain your thought process. How familiar are you with algorithms?
